About Rcew

Rajasthan College of Engineering for Women (RCEW) was established in the year 2002 by Chandrawati Education Society. The college offers 4-year full-time B.Tech course in four specializations, 3-year MCA course, 2-year MBA course in 3 specializations and the M.Tech course in 3 specializations.

  • The application form is common for all courses and is available online on the website of the college.
  • Candidates who have passed their 10+2 or equivalent examination with Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry from a recognized Board are eligible for admission to B.Tech program.
  • The candidates who hold a Bachelorâ€™s Degree in any discipline from a recognized university are eligible for admission to MCA and MBA programs.
  • BE/ B.Tech Degree with valid GATE score is required for admission to the M.Tech program.
  • Selection of the candidates for B.Tech course is done through Rajasthan Engineering Admission Process (REAP).
  • Selection of the candidates for MCA course is done on the basis of Rajasthan State Centralized Counselling for Master of Computer Application (RMCAAT).
  • Admission of the eligible candidates to MBA course is done through CMAT.
